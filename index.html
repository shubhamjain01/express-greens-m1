Our Commitments

What we pledge to deliver — infrastructure, security, sustainability, community welfare, and governance.

🏗️

Restoration & Structural Integrity

  • Whitewash & Repainting: Complete whitewashing, repainting, and external restoration of all towers with a professional structural and safety audit.
  • Eradicating Seepage: Flat-by-flat resolution of seepage and leakage issues through expert, long-lasting repairs.
🔧

Campus Upgrades & Quality Control

  • Roads & Utilities: Immediate repair of internal roads; all electrical and plumbing works under the new RWA's scope.
  • Resident-Led Inspections: Form an inspection team for maintenance work, submitting transparent quarterly quality reports.
⚖️

Legal Advocacy & Accountability

  • Recovering CAM Dues: Advocate-led recovery of full CAM dues from HSIIDC-owned flats with documented follow-ups.
  • Clubhouse & Boundary Walls: Ensure world-class clubhouse and amenities via HSIIDC/Govt, enforcing Supreme Court's MA50/2019 Judgment.
  • Escalation Protocol: Structured records of HSIIDC-transferred units; unresolved obligations escalated to DTCP, HRERA, and other authorities.
🛡️

Unwavering Security

  • Access Control: Strengthen entry protocols, install modern access-control systems, and expand CCTV to all blind spots.
  • Rapid Response: Form a dedicated emergency team (including 2 guards) for round-the-clock protection.
  • Zero Tolerance: Strictly restrict unlawful activities to ensure safety for children, women, and seniors.
🚨

Emergency Preparedness

  • Life-Saving Setup: Emergency alarm switch in every flat and a 24x7 medical ambulance on-site.
  • Disaster Readiness: Fire drills, CPR training for volunteers, fully stocked first-aid kits, and a 24/7 resident helpline.
⚡

Eco-Friendly Future

  • EV & Solar: Install EV charging stations, explore solar power solutions, and strengthen rainwater harvesting.
  • LED Transition: Switch all common-area lighting to LED to cut energy costs.
  • Green Commute: Introduce a prepaid bicycle-sharing system (like TILT) for fitness and eco-friendly mobility.
🌳

Cleanliness & Green Spaces

  • Waste Revolution: Garbage collection through MCM/HSIIDC, 5-star housekeeping SOPs, composting, and recycling drives.
  • Green Spaces: Beautify the main gate, mini-park between A, B & C, D towers, and develop the green belt outside campus.
💊

Health & Wellness

  • Health First: Monthly free health check-up camps with local clinics; promote yoga and wellness clubs.
  • Childhood Joy: Upgrade kids' play areas with modern, durable equipment and soft-fall surfaces.
🎉

Community & Culture

  • Vibrant Culture: Organize festivals, cultural programs, sports days, and music nights for inter-tower unity. Venues for meetings/parties until the Clubhouse is built.
  • Spiritual Space: Secure approvals for a small, inclusive prayer space for spiritual gatherings and meditation.
🏛️

Governance & Digital Empowerment

  • App-Based Management: Digital resident portal/WhatsApp for real-time updates, online billing, facility bookings, and community polls.
  • 48-Hour Resolution: Guaranteed grievance resolution timeline with transparent digital tracking.
  • Financial Integrity: Full transparency in collections, contractor payments, and audits through open AGMs. Zero tolerance for financial opacity.
